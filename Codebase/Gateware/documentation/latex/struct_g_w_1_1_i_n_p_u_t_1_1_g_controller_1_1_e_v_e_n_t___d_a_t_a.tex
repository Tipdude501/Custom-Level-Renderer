\mbox{\hyperlink{struct_g_w_1_1_i_n_p_u_t_1_1_g_controller_1_1_e_v_e_n_t___d_a_t_a}{E\+V\+E\+N\+T\+\_\+\+D\+A\+TA}} holds the information about a controller event. See \mbox{\hyperlink{group___g_controller_codes}{G\+Controller\+Codes}} for a list of input\+Code defines.
