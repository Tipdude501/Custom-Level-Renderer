171  * The volume ratio in _values for _numChannels amount of channels.

182  * INDEX[4] = Rear Left,
183  * INDEX[5] = Rear Right,
184  *
185  * \param [in] _values The array of volumes per channel (size MUST match the passed in _numChannels)
186  * \param [in] _numChannels The number of channels affected.
